IMPACT ON ME;

My father is a man like no other. He has loved me unconditionally and through this I've learnt how to be selfless and mindful of others.

He has taught me to look out for others and to be calm in the face of adversity.

He never misses a workout, he always nourishes his body with the best that nature provides, he never skips a doctor's appointment, and through that I've been able to learn to prioritize health and take care of my body.

He has also encouraged me to be disciplined, intentional and consistent with anything and everything I do. I can't tell you how much I appreciate that.

APPRECIATION;

Thank you for all the sacrifices you have made, all the support you gave me when I needed it most and moulding me into the person I have become today.I adore you with all my heart and I'm proud to be yours.
